Space marine

Click here for full image

This model is made for my RTS project, as a first example of what the level of detail I'm aiming for in that game.

For the sake of this showcase, I've made coloring to the textures. However in in my game project I plan on making all of these textures white, so the player can customize their own army scheme in any way they want.

Click play to go 360 around the model

I've been making solid progress with my skills in blender, now being very comfortable working on different kinds of shapes and sizes.

Texturing is something I'm still learning. I've added the basic bump- & normal, and roughness maps, as well as some nice little details, like the imperfections on the main metal textures. However I still want to learn more, to make the metals look more dirty in some places, which I simply haven't tried yet.
